HermesWiki is a centralized documentation repository designed to support users of the Hermes autonomous AI email agent. It serves as a knowledge base that provides structured guidance on implementing and managing the agent's core functionalities. The resource offers specific deployment patterns and practical recipes to help users configure the system for their specific needs. By consolidating these technical instructions, the project ensures that developers have a reliable reference for optimizing their AI-driven email workflows.
